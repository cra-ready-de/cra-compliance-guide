# CRA Timeline and Deadlines: What Manufacturers Need to Know by 2027

## CRA Timeline and Deadlines: What Manufacturers Need to Know by 2027
The Cyber Resilience Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/2847) entered into force on 11 December 2024. For manufacturers of products with digital elements, implementation follows a phased logic: individual organisational and regulatory preparatory stages apply earlier, while the full product requirements apply from the end of 2027.

## Corrigendum (EU) 2025/327 of 5 March 2025
Corrigendum (EU) 2025/327 clarifies how to deal with areas that fall under EHDS-related health data rules. For affected manufacturers, the delineation between CRA obligations and sector-specific health data requirements is central.

## Corrigendum 2025/90555 of 2 July 2025
Corrigendum 2025/90555 further specifies, among other points, the interpretation of Art.64(10) with regard to fine exemptions for micro and SME constellations. Companies should not assume these exemption areas as a blanket rule, but should review them in a documented manner.

## Implementing Regulation (EU) 2025/2392 of 28 November 2025
Implementing Regulation (EU) 2025/2392 sharpens product classification and supports the consistent allocation of products to CRA classes. For practical implementation, this is particularly important for scope definition, conformity assessment and documentation.
