# CRA Compliance Guide

**Multilingual compliance documentation for the EU Cyber Resilience Act
(Regulation EU 2024/2847)**

Maintained by **[CRA Ready](https://www.cra-ready.de)** —
the B2B SaaS platform helping German Mittelstand manufacturers
achieve CRA compliance.

---

## What is this?

This repository contains structured compliance documentation
for manufacturers of products with digital elements (PDEs)
subject to the EU Cyber Resilience Act.

All articles are derived from the official Help Center at
[cra-ready.de](https://www.cra-ready.de) and are available
in 6 languages.

## Topics Covered

- **CRA Fundamentals** — scope, product classification, timelines
- **SBOM** — requirements, formats (CycloneDX, SPDX)
- **Vulnerability Management** — Art. 14 reporting obligations,
  24h/72h deadlines, security.txt
- **Conformity Assessment** — CE marking, conformity routes
- **CVE News** — analysis of actively exploited vulnerabilities
  relevant to CRA Art. 14

---

## Key Deadlines

| Date | Obligation |
|---|---|
| **11 September 2026** | Art. 14 vulnerability reporting active (ENISA SRP) |
| **11 December 2027** | Full CRA enforcement — all manufacturers |

## About CRA Ready

[CRA Ready](https://www.cra-ready.de) is a B2B SaaS compliance
platform built specifically for German industrial Mittelstand
manufacturers. It covers the complete CRA compliance workflow:
product registration, SBOM management, vulnerability handling,
risk assessment, technical documentation, conformity assessment,
CE marking, and post-market surveillance.
